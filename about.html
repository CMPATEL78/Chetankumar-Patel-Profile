Profile Summary

Academic leader and Professor with 23+ years of experience in Higher Education Institutions and 2 years of industry exposure, demonstrating strong capabilities in academic governance, teaching–learning innovation, research coordination, and quality assurance. Proven expertise in academic planning, curriculum design, Outcome-Based Education (OBE), and implementation of learner-centric pedagogies such as Problem-Based Learning (PBL), Project-Based Learning, and blended learning using LMS platforms.

Played key leadership roles in NAAC and NIRF accreditation and ranking processes. Led a robust research and innovation ecosystem with 30+ funded projects, 19 patents, and 80 research publications, promoting IPR generation, applied research, and industry-aligned outcomes. Experienced in faculty mentoring, doctoral guidance, startup nurturing, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Authored and co-authored three books reflecting a systems-driven approach to academic excellence, pedagogy, and leadership.
